BLOCKCHAIN AUDIT TRAIL REQUIREMENTS
Document ID: KB-BAR-012
Effective Date: 2025-09-01
Regulatory Basis: FSMA Section 204 (Food Traceability Rule); EIP-2535 Diamond Standard

1. DECISION RECORD STRUCTURE

Every routing decision generated by the multi-agent system must be recorded as an
immutable audit trail entry containing the following fields: UTC timestamp of decision,
agent identifier, action taken (cold chain, redistribution, animal feed, composting),
spoilage index rho at decision time, SLCA composite score, carbon intensity value,
and free-text justification summarizing the reasoning basis.

Each record is serialized and hashed using keccak256 to produce a unique decision
fingerprint. The fingerprint is stored on-chain as part of the transaction payload,
while the full record is stored off-chain in the cooperative document management system
with a pointer linking the two.

2. RETENTION AND TRACEABILITY

All decision audit records must be retained for a minimum of 2 years from the date of
creation, consistent with FSMA Section 204 traceability record retention requirements.
Records must be retrievable by lot number, agent ID, date range, or action type within
4 hours of a regulatory inquiry.

3. GOVERNANCE AND VOTING

Significant policy changes, including modifications to routing thresholds, SLCA weights,
or diversion criteria, require approval through the on-chain governance mechanism. A
quorum of 3 agents from distinct organizational roles must participate in the vote.
The voting window is 24 hours from proposal submission. Proposals that do not achieve
quorum within the voting window are automatically tabled for re-submission.

4. REQUIRED ACTIONS

Action 1: Implement automated integrity verification that re-computes the keccak256
hash for each stored record and compares it against the on-chain fingerprint. Run this
verification daily and report any mismatches to the cooperative security officer within
1 hour of detection.

Action 2: Conduct semi-annual governance audits per EIP-2535 modular contract standards
to verify that all proxy upgrades, facet additions, and parameter changes were approved
through the required quorum process. Document findings and submit to the cooperative
governance board within 30 days of audit completion.
