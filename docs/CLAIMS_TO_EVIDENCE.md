# Claims to Evidence Map

This document maps manuscript claims to reproducible artifacts generated by the canonical pipeline (`python mvp/simulation/reproduce_core.py`).

## Core Claims

| Claim ID | Manuscript claim | Primary evidence artifacts | Validation rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | AGRI-BRAIN outperforms context baselines across core scenarios on ARI/SLCA and reduces waste/carbon | `mvp/simulation/results/benchmark_summary.json`, `mvp/simulation/results/benchmark_significance.json`, `mvp/simulation/results/paper_benchmark_table.json` | Check mean differences and paired stats per scenario/baseline/metric |
| C2 | Gains remain under multi-seed uncertainty, not single-seed variance | `mvp/simulation/results/benchmark_summary.json` | `n=20` seed policy and 95% CIs present for reported metrics |

| C4 | Context interoperability (MCP + piRAG) contributes to quality improvements | `mvp/simulation/results/benchmark_significance.json`, `mvp/simulation/results/mcp_protocol_*.json`, `mvp/simulation/results/mcp_interop_*.json` | Compare `agribrain` vs `mcp_only`, `pirag_only`, `no_context`; inspect protocol traces |
| C5 | Robustness under stress perturbations is bounded and auditable | `mvp/simulation/results/stress_summary.json`, `mvp/simulation/results/stress_degradation.csv`, `mvp/simulation/results/stress_passfail.csv` | Verify pass/fail threshold compliance and absolute+delta reporting |

| C7 | Results are reproducible and provenance-pinned | `mvp/simulation/results/artifact_manifest.json`, `mvp/simulation/results/_pipeline_logs/pipeline_status.json` | Manifest includes SHA-256 hashes and exact `git_commit` |
| C8 | Figures reflect benchmark uncertainty where applicable | `mvp/simulation/results/fig6_cross.*`, `fig7_ablation.*`, `fig8_green_ai.*`, `fig9_mcp_pirag_robustness.*`, `fig10_latency_quality_frontier.*` | CI bars/uncertainty overlays visible in final rendered plots |
